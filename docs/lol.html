lol_print#

Prints a list of lists in a tabulated way

from pydicts import lol
lol_=[[1, 2, 3], [4, 5, 6]]
lol.lol_print(lol_)

+---+---+---+
| 1 | 2 | 3 |
| 4 | 5 | 6 |
+---+---+---+

lol_transposed#

Returns a trasnsposed list of lists

from pydicts.lol import lol_transposed
lol_=[[1, 2, 3], [4, 5, 6]]
lol.lol_print(lol_)
transposed=lol.lol_transposed(lol_)
lol.lol_print(transposed)

+---+---+---+
| 1 | 2 | 3 |
| 4 | 5 | 6 |
+---+---+---+
+---+---+
| 1 | 4 |
| 2 | 5 |
| 3 | 6 |
+---+---+
